Installing rOpenSci packages

There are two ways to do this. Installing from CRAN, or from GitHub. Install one of our packages from CRAN:


$ install.packages("rplos")
$ library(rplos)

Or install one of our packages from GitHub:


$ install_github('ropensnp', 'ropensci')
$ library(ropensnp)
